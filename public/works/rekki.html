REKKI Market

Product Design Lead, 2019–

REKKI is an app for chefs to order directly from their suppliers. Our mission is to help independent restaurants stay sustainable by making it easier to manage food costs, stock, and orders.

Founded by chefs and determined to make the work of back-of-house restaurant staff easier, REKKI focuses on a userbase that has until now received little attention from tech. While much of society has moved from phone calls to email and messaging for most comunication, the restaurant industry is still run over the phone: the typical restaurant still orders the next day's ingredients over voice mails every night, a process that is prone to mistakes and misunderstandings.
